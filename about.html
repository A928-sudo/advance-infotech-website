About Advance Infotech

Director & Founder

Mr.Jayant Pohane

Mr.Jayant Pohane is a seasoned technology expert and educator with over 28 years of experience in the fields of information technology, software development, and education. With a passion for bridging the gap between technology and education, Mr. Pohane founded Advance Computer Education in 1995 with a clear goal: to provide students with the practical knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the ever-evolving digital world.

What is Advance Computer Education (ACE)?

Advance Computer Education (ACE) is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality education in the fields of Information Technology, Computer Science, and Design. Established with a vision to bridge the gap between the growing technological needs and educational standards, ACE is committed to offering practical, hands-on learning experiences. We aim to equip students with the necessary technical skills and knowledge to excel in their careers and contribute to the ever-evolving digital world.

At ACE, we focus on providing a comprehensive education that includes both theory and practical application. Our expert faculty members, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry-relevant courses ensure that students are prepared for success in the global IT industry.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in the ever-changing technological landscape, providing them with a solid foundation for personal and professional growth.

Our Mission

We aim to offer high-quality, accessible, and affordable education in the fields of IT and design, while fostering creativity and critical thinking to equip our students for success in the digital world.
